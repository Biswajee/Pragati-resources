Why love Pragati ?


Pragati is an intuitive app that unites our richer and poor class of society together. It is an application aimed to erradicate food crisis in society and in general, throughout the country by bringing closer the richer class to empathize with the poor and thereby donating additional food to the needy to make their day great.
