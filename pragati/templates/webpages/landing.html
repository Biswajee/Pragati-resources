Pragati | Serve the needy.

Reach out the needy in your society within clicks. Help them have a great day by donating your kitchen leftovers, Canteen leftovers, etc so that none of your society dwellers have to sleep with an empty stomach.
Erradicate Hunger. Spread Love.

Download App

EASY TO USE

Pragati is fast and elegant in noting your kitchen leftovers. Its easy UI/UX makes the application handling easy and intuitive. For more insights of reviews. Read our testimonials section.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION

Pragati has fast and low storage Integration using Firebase Realtime Database (a noSQL database) that allows faster updation of data. Firebase Authentication lessens the hassle in maintaining and storing password.

DATA ECONOMY

Pragati app values its donors and understands the value of internet data on mobile devices. Thus, it only uses a minimum amount of data each time a transaction is made and no data transfer otherwise. This make the donor community stronger.
